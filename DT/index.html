Define: The Core Challenge

Problem Statement

How might we create a unified multimodal platform that empowers travelers to seamlessly plan, compare, and book journeys across flights, trains, and buses while optimizing for their personal time vs. cost preferences?

Connectivity

Must bridge gaps between major hubs and tier-2/3 cities.

Reliability

Must account for transfer buffers and delays.

Usability

Must simplify complex data into clear choices.

Ideate: Solutions

Algorithmic Solutions

Graph Routing (A*)

Treats cities as nodes and transport links as edges. Weights edges by cost AND time.

Transfer Penalty

Adds a "cost" to transfers to prefer direct routes unless savings are significant.

Experience Solutions

Preference Slider

UI control to weight "Time" vs "Money" dynamically.

Unified Ticket View

Visualizing the whole journey as a single timeline.
